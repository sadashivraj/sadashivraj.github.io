About Me

I'm a builder at heart. While others integrate existing solutions, I architect and construct entire platforms from the ground up.

My work spans the full spectrum of AI and data engineering—from designing scalable data pipelines to building intelligent systems that process millions of data points. I believe in understanding systems at their core, which is why I build from scratch rather than patching together third-party solutions.

When I see a complex problem, I see an opportunity to create something elegant and powerful. This approach has led me to build three major platforms: Phoenix, AIDE, and GenAI—each solving unique challenges in the AI and data space.
